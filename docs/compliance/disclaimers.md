# Training Disclaimers

User-facing disclaimer text for Sthir. Display during intake; store acceptance in `answers.disclaimerAccepted`.

## Primary disclaimer (intake checkbox)

> **Training disclaimer:** Sthir provides general strength training programming based on information you provide. This is **not** medical advice, physical therapy, or a substitute for consultation with a qualified healthcare provider.
>
> You should consult a physician before beginning any exercise program, especially if you have pre-existing injuries, cardiovascular conditions, or are pregnant.
>
> You assume full responsibility for your training decisions. Stop exercising and seek medical attention if you experience pain, dizziness, or unusual symptoms.
>
> Sthir coaches review programs for training appropriateness but cannot guarantee competition results or injury prevention.
>
> By proceeding, you confirm you are **18 years or older** (or have guardian consent) and accept these terms.

## Short form (footer / email)

> Sthir programs are for educational purposes. Train at your own risk. Not medical advice.

## Competition / federation disclaimer

> Federation rules vary by organization and meet. Sthir provides general guidance — verify rules with your meet director. Sthir is not affiliated with IPF, Powerlifting India, WRPF, or any governing body unless explicitly stated.

## Injury-specific note (coach notes template)

> If you experience pain beyond normal muscle soreness, reduce load by 10–20% and consult a sports medicine professional before continuing.

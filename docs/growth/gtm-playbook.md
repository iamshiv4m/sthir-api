# GTM Playbook — Phase 1

Go-to-market strategy for Sthir months 0–6.

## Positioning

**One line:** Your personalized powerlifting program — reviewed by a real coach — delivered in 12 hours.

**India wedge:** Federation-aware (IPF/PI, WRPF), warehouse gym culture, UPI payments, ₹499–1,499 price band.

**Not competing on:** Free tracking (Hevy/Boostcamp), AI-only programming (JuggernautAI).

## Phase 1 channels (priority order)

### 1. Warehouse gym partnerships

- Target: 5 gyms in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore
- Offer: QR poster, referral code, ₹100 athlete discount
- Gym gets: ₹100–200 per conversion or 10% rev share
- Activation: mock meet day, lifter spotlight
- Track: [gym-partners.md](gym-partners.md)

### 2. Meet-day presence

- Micro-sponsorship: ₹5–10k best lifter prize
- QR on meet banner → landing with `?ref=meet_[name]`
- Founder attends 2 meets/month for credibility

### 3. Instagram Reels

- Content: meet stories, athlete results, gym culture — **not** feature demos
- Post 3–4×/week
- CTA: link in bio → `/intake`

### 4. OpenPowerlifting SEO

- State ranking pages: "Best lifters in [State] 2026"
- Link to Sthir programming CTA
- Long-term organic — start month 2

### 5. Coach referral

- 15% rev share for referring coaches (Phase 1 manual tracking)
- Target: 10 coach interviews (E6 experiment)

## Launch sequence

| Week | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1–2 | E1: Landing + waitlist + ₹99 deposit |
| 2–4 | E2: 10 concierge manual programs |
| 4–6 | E3: Price A/B test |
| 4–8 | E4: Gym poster vs Meta ads |
| 5–10 | MVP launch with gym partners live |

## CAC targets

| Channel | Target CAC |
|---------|------------|
| Gym referral | < ₹200 |
| Organic/meet | < ₹100 |
| Meta ads | < ₹400 |
| Blended | < ₹400 by month 6 |

**Kill:** CAC > ₹800 with no organic by month 3.

## Meet-day checklist

- [ ] QR posters printed (A3, gym + meet venue)
- [ ] Referral codes loaded for partner gyms
- [ ] Founder business cards / WhatsApp QR
- [ ] Phone charged for UPI demo
- [ ] Athlete testimonial ask script ready
- [ ] Photo consent for social post

## Messaging do's and don'ts

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| "Reviewed by a real coach" | "AI-powered" as headline |
| Federation names (neutral) | Take sides in PI/IPF dispute |
| Real meet results | Stock photos / fake testimonials |
| 12h delivery promise | "Instant" delivery |

## Metrics dashboard (weekly)

- Waitlist signups / deposits
- Paid programs / week
- CAC by channel
- SLA compliance
- NPS
- Gym partner referrals

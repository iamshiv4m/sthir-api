# Product Vision

**Sthir** — India's strength sports platform, evolving toward a global operating system for barbell sports.

## Mission

Give every Indian lifter access to **trustworthy, federation-aware programming** — not generic app templates — with the speed of software and the accountability of a real coach.

## Year 1 belief

- **Moat order:** Trust → Data → Network
- **Year 1 wedge:** Founder coaching credibility + 12h human-reviewed delivery
- **Code is not the moat** in Phase 1; programming quality and local gym network are

## Phased roadmap

| Phase | Theme | Core deliverable | Gate to next phase |
|-------|-------|------------------|-------------------|
| **1** | Programming-as-a-Service | Questionnaire → template → coach review → Sheet/PDF | 80+ paid programs, NPS ≥40, 90% SLA |
| **2** | Tracker | Web workout log, RPE/RIR, PR history, sheet import | 80+ paid programs delivered |
| **3** | Community | Profiles, meets, leaderboards, clubs | 1k+ registered, tracker WAU |
| **4** | Coach marketplace | Discovery, subscriptions, payouts | Density in 3+ cities |
| **5** | Event OS | Meet registration, federation adapters | Partnership + legal ready |

## Phase 1 promise

> Your personalized powerlifting program — reviewed by a real coach — delivered in **12 hours**.

## North-star metrics (Year 1)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Revenue | ₹30–80L |
| Paid programs | 80–150 |
| Registered users | 1,000 |
| NPS | ≥ 40 |
| SLA compliance | ≥ 90% within 12h |
| Week-4 adherence | ≥ 40% (concierge beta) |

## Kill criteria (Month 3)

- <30 paid programs
- NPS < 20
- CAC > ₹800 with no organic channel

## What we reject (Phase 1)

- Launching as "another workout tracker"
- Full AI autoprogramming without human review
- Community/forums before user density
- Waiting for federation government recognition before shipping

## Federation scope

Multi-federation from day one: **IPF/Powerlifting India (PI)** primary, **WRPF India** parallel, extensible to others.

## Internal vs external narrative

- **External:** India strength sports ecosystem
- **Internal (until 100 deliveries):** "Program factory" — narrow focus, excellent delivery
